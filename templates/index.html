LunarLIGHT
From Shadows to Sight, Exploring the Moon's Poles
What We Do
Our LunarLIGHT project is dedicated to enhancing the visibility and quality of images taken from low-light environments, such as the moon's poles. Using advanced image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms, we transform shadowed and low-contrast images into clear, detailed visuals, providing invaluable insights and data. From improving image clarity to enabling detailed analysis, our solution pushes the boundaries of space exploration imaging.
